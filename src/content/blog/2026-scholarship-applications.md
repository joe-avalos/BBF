We are pleased to announce that applications for the 2026-2027 BBF scholarship cycle are now open. Each year, Building Baja's Future awards full-tuition scholarships to qualifying students enrolled in bachelor's degree programs at universities throughout Baja California Sur.

## Who Can Apply

To be considered for a BBF scholarship, applicants must meet the following requirements:

- Demonstrated financial need, verified through a socioeconomic assessment
- Minimum high school GPA of 9.0 out of 10
- Active participation in school and community activities
- Enrollment in a bachelor's degree program at an institution within Baja California Sur

## How to Apply

1. **Download the application form** from the BBF office or request one in person.
2. **Complete all sections** in black ink or typed. Sign and date the form.
3. **Attach required documents**: a passport-size photo and your official high school transcript showing a GPA of 9.0 or higher.
4. **Include a teacher recommendation letter** from a current or former instructor.
5. **Deliver your complete application** to the BBF office. Office hours: Monday–Friday 9:00–16:00, Saturday 9:00–14:00.

Selected candidates will be invited to an in-person interview and aptitude evaluation.

## Key Dates

- **Application deadline**: March 30, 2026
- **Award notifications**: By June 15, 2026
- **Scholarship period begins**: August 2026

## What the Scholarship Covers

BBF scholarships cover **100% of college tuition** for the duration of the scholar's degree program, provided academic and program requirements are maintained. Scholars also gain access to BBF's full support model: English classes, mentorship, community service coordination, health and wellness programs, and more.

If you have questions about the application process, please contact the BBF office directly. We look forward to welcoming the next cohort of scholars into the BBF community.
