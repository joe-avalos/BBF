As we enter 2026, we want to take a moment to reflect on everything the BBF community accomplished in 2025. None of it would be possible without our scholars, donors, mentors, and partners.

## Scholars

- **38 active scholars** supported throughout the 2024-2025 academic year
- **12 scholars graduated** with bachelor's degrees, bringing our all-time total to 348+ alumni
- **9 new scholars** welcomed into the program in August 2025

Our scholars are enrolled in programs ranging from nursing and education to computer science, law, and environmental engineering — across universities in La Paz, Los Cabos, and Ciudad Constitución.

## Programs

### English Instruction
Over 750 hours of English instruction were delivered across weekly group sessions. Six scholars achieved intermediate-level proficiency by year's end, opening doors to broader professional opportunities.

### Community Service
BBF scholars collectively contributed more than 1,200 hours of community service in 2025. Projects included environmental clean-ups, tutoring programs for younger students, and support for local health fairs.

### Internships
Eight scholars participated in professional internship placements through BBF's work program. Host organizations included local hospitality groups, engineering firms, and public health agencies.

### Wellness
Yoga and swimming sessions continued throughout the year with strong participation. Six scholars utilized BBF's access to professional psychological counseling — a program that continues to grow in response to scholar needs.

## Financials

BBF is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. In 2025, scholarship tuition coverage accounted for the largest share of program expenditures. BBF is grateful for the continued generosity of individual donors, corporate sponsors, and community partners who make this possible.

A full financial summary is available upon request.

## Looking to 2026

Applications for the 2026-2027 academic year are now open through March 30th. We anticipate supporting 10–12 new scholars in the incoming cohort.

Thank you to everyone who contributed to BBF's mission in 2025. Every dollar, every hour mentored, and every connection made moves us closer to a stronger Baja California Sur.
