Page Map — EN (Donors) vs ES (Applicants)

Which pages are shared (with different focus), which are unique to each language?

🇺🇸 English — Donor-Focused

Shared pages (donor tone)

  • 🏠 Home — lead with impact, donation CTA
  • 📖 Our Story — mission, credibility, 501(c)(3)
  • 🤝 How We Help — "what your money funds"
  • 🎓 Scholars — success stories, proof of impact
  • 📰 Newsletter — latest + archive

EN-only pages

  • 💰 How to Donate — PayPal, check, methods
  • 🌟 Friends of BBF — donor recognition
  • 📊 Your Impact — where donations go, transparency

NOT in English

  • 📝 How to Apply
  • 📚 Student Resources
🇲🇽 Spanish — Applicant-Focused

Shared pages (applicant tone)

  • 🏠 Inicio — lead with opportunity, apply CTA
  • 📖 Quiénes Somos — mission, A.C., SAT compliance
  • 🤝 Cómo Ayudamos — "what you receive as a scholar"
  • 🎓 Becarios — peer role models, "people like you"
  • 📰 Boletín — latest + archive

ES-only pages

  • 📝 Cómo Obtener la Beca — application process, requirements
  • 📚 Recursos para Becarios — obligations, support, FAQ

NOT in Spanish

  • 💰 How to Donate
  • 🌟 Friends of BBF
  • 📊 Your Impact

Navigation implication

The nav menu would be different per language. EN nav shows donate-oriented links, ES nav shows applicant-oriented links. The language toggle switches you to the other audience's version of the site, not a translation of the same page.
